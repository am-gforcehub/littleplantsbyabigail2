Pumpkin Seeds

Once your seeds germinate and turn into sprouts in your pot they are ready to plant into the ground.

Best time to grow pumpkins are in late May to late July.

If planting more than one, make sure you place each sprout 4 to 8 feet apart.

Pumpkins are thirsty, make sure you water them often.

Remember to keep pumpkins warm, they love the sun.

Your pumpkins will be ready to harvest in 130-160 days.
